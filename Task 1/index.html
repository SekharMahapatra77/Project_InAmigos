About InAmigos Foundation


InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization founded in 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla. The organization works across India to create positive social impact through education, food support, women empowerment, environmental conservation, animal welfare, and skill development programs.

Our Ongoing Projects

Project Bachpanshala

Providing educational support, digital literacy, and learning opportunities to underprivileged children.

Project Seva

Distributing food, clothing, and essential resources to people in need.

Project Jeev

Supporting stray animals through feeding, shelter, care, and welfare activities.

Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development, awareness programs, and financial independence initiatives.

Project Prakriti

Promoting environmental sustainability through tree plantation and conservation drives.

Project Vikas

Providing internships and skill development opportunities to improve employability.

Social Impact


  • 50,000+ beneficiaries supported.
  • Food and clothing distributed to vulnerable communities.
  • Educational opportunities for underprivileged children.
  • Women empowered through training and awareness programs.
  • Environmental conservation through plantation drives.
  • Care and support for stray animals.
